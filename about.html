About Us

About Us

Achyuta Welfare Foundation is a section 8 registered non-profit organization emerging foundation which goals for a betterment of society. It aims to the betterment of the society in multiple domains like Healthcare, Education, Women Empowerment, Animal Welfare, Micro and Small Businesses and others. In our NGO, we also aim for a sustainable future. At Achyuta Welfare Foundation, we strive to create a better tomorrow by addressing some of the most pressing challenges of today. Driven by compassion, innovation, and unwavering commitment, we focus on six core domains to bring meaningful change to society.

  • Empowering young professionals and students in Dehradun.
  • Dedicated community services and fellowship.
  • Addressing pressing social issues through diverse projects.
  • Promoting education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.
  • Transforming lives and shaping a better future.
  • Fostering leadership development and cultural exchange.
  • A beacon of hope and services in the heritage of the Himalayas.

Helpful

Empowering individuals and communities lies at the heart of what we do. From offering quality education to underprivileged children to ensuring access to essential healthcare, we believe in uplifting lives through knowledge and care.

Dedicated

Our dedication extends to women empowerment, enabling them to achieve financial independence and self-reliance. Through skill-building initiatives, advocacy, and awareness programs, we are transforming the lives of countless women across communities.

Perfect People

No action is too small to create a ripple effect of change. By working tirelessly in animal welfare, promoting small businesses through microservices, and conserving our environment, we aim to inspire everyone to contribute towards building a sustainable and compassionate future.
