Frequently Asked Questions

What is Achyuta Welfare Foundation? +
Achyuta Welfare Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through various initiatives, including education, health, and social welfare programs.
How can I donate to your foundation? +
You can donate to our foundation by visiting our "Donate" page. We accept various modes of donations, including online payments, bank transfers, and in-kind contributions.
Are donations to Achyuta Welfare Foundation tax-deductible? +
Yes, donations made to Achyuta Welfare Foundation are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.
How can I volunteer for your initiatives? +
You can sign up as a volunteer by filling out the volunteer form on our "Team" page. We welcome individuals who share our vision and want to contribute to making a difference.
Where are your services currently available? +
Our services are currently available in Dehradun, Bhopal, and other selected cities across India. We aim to expand our reach in the near future.
