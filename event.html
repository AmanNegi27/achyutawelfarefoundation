Cloth Distribution drive -1, Bhopal

WARMTH OF KINDNESS SPREADS IN BHOPAL Bhopal, Dec 1, 2024: On a chilly winter morning, Team Bhopal of Achyuta Welfare Foundation brought warmth and smiles to the underprivileged at Chetak Bridge. The foundation's cloth distribution drive was a beacon of hope, reminding us that small gestures can make a big difference. Beneficiaries' eyes sparkled with gratitude, with one saying, "This is a blessing for us. We're thankful to Achyuta Welfare Foundation for thinking of us." A shining example of Bhopal's spirit of kindness and generosity.

Cloth Distribution drive -2, Bhopal

The Achyuta Welfare Foundation recently organized a cloth distribution drive in Kalpana Nagar, BHEL Bhopal. The foundation distributed new and usable clothing items to underprivileged families, aiming to provide warmth and comfort during the winter season. The event was well-received by the local community, with many expressing gratitude to the foundation.

Collaboration with KFC

KFC RAJPUR DEHRADUN GETS A HEARTWARMING INAUGURATION Dehradun: In a unique gesture, KFC Rajpur Dehradun and Achyuta Welfare Foundation joined hands to have 12 slum children inaugurate the new outlet. The children received a warm welcome, veg burgers, fries, gifts, and enjoyed creative activities. This innovative inauguration marked a new culinary journey while highlighting the importance of giving back to the community. Kudos to KFC Rajpur Dehradun and Achyuta Welfare Foundation for spreading joy and warmth!
